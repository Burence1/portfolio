About Me

Burens is a 23 year old kenyan with a high affinity for technology. Being a software Developer has been my child hood dream despite the hiccups experienced in the quest to achieve this feat. Moreover, I commenced my academic journey at Bethel primary (Homabay) before proceeding to St. Peter’s Cape View,Homabay. Consequently, I proceeded to St. Mary’s School,Yala for my post primary education. Well, it was at this institution that I developed a great thirst for technology alongside finding an academic and tech mentor(Collins Reuben, currently a Full stack developer). My mentor aided in transforming my child hood dream into a reality. I proceeded to join Chuka University where I pursued a Bsc. in Computer Science and graduated in 2020. In a bid to develop my skills and a partial fulfillment of my Degree, I was attached at the Milimani Law Courts for a period between 09/May/2019 to 19/August/2019. Moreover, I sharpened my skills and gained skills in relation to computer hardware and software. Considering my unending thirst for technology knowledge, I joined Moringa School on 02, February,2021 a community am happy to be a part of.
